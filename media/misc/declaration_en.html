Ziele der Untersuchung

Im folgenden Experiment untersuchen wir die Steuerung visuell-räumlicher Aufmerksamkeit und die Wahrnehmung von Zeit.

Ablauf und Dauer der Untersuchung

In jedem Durchgang dieses Experiments sehen Sie ein Muster aus Strichen. Zwei der Striche sind farbig (je rot und grün). Am Anfang jedes Durchgangs hören Sie eine Anweisung. Diese sagt Ihnen, welcher Strich für die weitere Aufgabe relevant ist. Anschließend werden beide farbigen Striche kurz blinken. Ihre Aufgabe ist es zu entscheiden, ob der in der Instruktion benannte Strich zuerst geblinkt hat oder als zweiter.
Es ist wichtig, dass Sie versuchen, uns möglichst exakt Ihren Wahrnehmungseindruck zu vermitteln bzw. möglichst genau zu reagieren. Den genauen Ablauf und weitere Details des Experimentes erfahren Sie kurz vor dem Experiment in einer weiteren Instruktion.

In regelmäßigen Abständen wird Ihnen eine Pause angeboten. Ein Block dauert – ohne Pausen – ca. 15 Minuten. Mehrere Blöcke sind erwünscht bzw. für den Erwerb von Versuchspersonenstunden in Ihrem Studium erforderlich. Beachten Sie bitte, dass die Untersuchung Konzentration erfordert.

Purpose of the study

In the following experiment we investigate the control of spatial visual attention and the perception of time.

Procedure and duration of the study

In each trial of this experiment, you will see a grid of bars. Two of these bars are colored (red or green) At the beginning of each trial, you will hear an instruction. It tells you which bar is relevant for the respective trial. Then, all colored bars will flash briefly. Your task is to decide whether the bar indicated by the instruction flashed first or second.
It is of great importance that you report your perceptions as accurately as possible or respond as accurate as possible. The exact details and instructions for the concrete task are given just before the start of the experiment in the form of a further instruction.

You will be offered a break at regular intervals. One block ("run through all trials") lasts – without breaks – about 15 minutes. Completing multiple passes / blocks is possible and welcomed. Participation in (multiple) passes can be credited for courses in your studies. Please note that the experiment requires concentration.

Advantages and conditions of participation

The primary function of this study is knowledge acquisition and will not provide you with any personal benefit. By participating in this study, you can obtain course credits in your studies. You may participate in the experiment as part of your work at PsyLab or at your own request without compensation.

Data protection information

We store and process

  • your age,
  • your gender,
  • the user agent of your browser,
  • your judgment on the order in the form of keystrokes within the study website,
  • your answer of the concluding survey.

We collect age and gender because they are typically reported in articles about experimental psychology studies; they are not used to analyze the data. We collect user agents to report on the devices used and, if necessary, to analyze errors that arise in connection with the various browsers and operating systems. The data are completely anonymized; they are stored on the computers of the researchers involved in the project until the study is completed. Later, they will be made publicly available on a data platform (e.g., Open Science https://osf.io) in case of publication; otherwise, they will be deleted after the retention period of 10 years. The data do not allow any conclusions to be drawn about your identity.

Furthermore, we store payment receipts, if compensation has been paid. The payment receipts are not assigned to your personal data record in the study.

If you would like to have your participation credited in your studies, please inform us of your participant code after the data collection for the purpose of querying and crediting the study participation time. Your personal information (name) will not be associated with your record.

Apart from the employees of the PsyLab and the SFB/TRR 318, third parties have no insight into personal data, insofar as they are collected. There will be no processing of third parties on behalf of us.
